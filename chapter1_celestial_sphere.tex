会合周期(synodic period)~$S$.
\begin{equation*}
    1/S = \left\lvert 1/P - 1/P_\oplus \right\rvert.
\end{equation*}

赤经(right ascension)~$\alpha$, 赤纬(declination)~$\delta$.
\begin{equation*}
    (\Delta\theta)^2 = (\Delta\alpha\cos\delta)^2+(\Delta\delta)^2
\end{equation*}
